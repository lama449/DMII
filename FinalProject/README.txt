Column - Description (Source of Data)

Date - The date the post was made (Webscraper)
Direction - 1 for an increase, -1 for decrease (Aggregated from Close/Last>Open in NASDAQ)
Stock - The stock ticker (Webscraper)
Sentiment - 1 for positive response, -1 for negative response provided by Textblob package (Webscraper)
Count - The number of posts made for a specific stock in a day (Aggregated from Webscraper)
